Gathering the Necessary Supplies

There are 5 things you will want for your oil change

  • 1. A new oil filter that fits your vehicle's engine
  • 2. Enough of your vehicles specific oil type to fill your engine to its manufacturer stated oil capacity
  • 3. A drain pan to catch the oil draining from your vehicles engine
  • 4. A funnel to make it easier pouring the oil into your vehicles engine
  • 5. A ratchet to remove, and re-tighten your engine's drain plug
  • 6. A jack to get your car high enough off the ground to reach the drain plug
