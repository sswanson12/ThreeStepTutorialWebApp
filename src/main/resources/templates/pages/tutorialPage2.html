Performing the Oil Change

This process can be extremely easy & quick, or time-consuming based on the age & condition of your vehicle

  • - Step 1 -
  • Lift your vehicle using the jack
  • - Step 2 -
  • Set oil pan underneath the drain plug
  • - Step 3 -
  • Remove drain plug using ratchet, and drain oil. (this may take some time, wait until the engine drips every 10 seconds or so)
  • - Step 4 -
  • Re-fasten drain plug in original spot using ratchet
  • - Step 5 -
  • Move oil pan underneath oil filter
  • - Step 6 -
  • Remove oil filter (should unscrew by hand)
  • - Step 8 -
  • Oil the gasket on your new filter for a proper seal, and hand-tighten the new filter into place
  • - Step 9 -
  • Fill your engine with oil. (The specified type and amount your vehicle requires)
  • - Step 10 -
  • Double check everything is fastened correctly, and start your engine to check for oil pressure!
  • - Step 11 -
  • If nothing bad happens, you did it right! Congrats!
